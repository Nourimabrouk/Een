    \[\phi = \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2} = 1.618033988749895...\]

    This transcendental number appears throughout nature and mathematics as the optimal ratio for growth, beauty, and harmony.

    Golden Properties¶

    • Self-similarity: \(\phi^2 = \phi + 1\)
    • Reciprocal: \(\frac{1}{\phi} = \phi - 1 = 0.618...\)
    • Unity Convergence: \(\phi^n + \phi^{-n} \rightarrow 1\) as operations compound
