Mathematical Statement

In Boolean algebra: TRUE ∨ TRUE = TRUE, hence 1 + 1 = 1

Proof Steps

Step 1: Let 1 represent TRUE in Boolean algebra
Step 2: Define OR operation (∨) as logical disjunction
Step 3: By definition, TRUE ∨ TRUE evaluates to TRUE
Step 4: Therefore, 1 ∨ 1 = 1 in Boolean algebra
Step 5: Hence, 1 + 1 = 1 when + represents OR operation
